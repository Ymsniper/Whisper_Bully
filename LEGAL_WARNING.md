## What This Does

Whisper_Bully extracts Bluetooth addresses from devices. The included l2flood
utility with the `-R` flag can then be used to stress-test those devices by
performing sustained L2CAP flooding. This is a **denial-of-service (DoS)
attack** that can:

- Lock devices out of Bluetooth connectivity
- Disrupt audio streams, device functionality
- Render devices unresponsive until the attack stops or the device resets
- Cause unexpected behavior or state corruption in the target
